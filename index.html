The Jeep Gladiator: Tearing Up The Trails With Ease! – The Perfect Overlanding Vehicle?

August 7, 2023

We carry on our showcasing of the top overlanding vehicles today. We have recently looked at the Toyota Tacoma and now we are going to focus on another top choice which is the Jeep Gladiator.

If you are looking for a vehicle that can do it all, which is also perfect for beginners and pros alike, then you will find it with the Jeep Gladiator. This vehicle is a firm favourite amongst our customers – let’s have a look at why that is below:

When you are taking an overlanding trip, one of the most important things is to make sure that you get a vehicle that is fit for the job.

You will find that the Jeep Gladiator gives you a perfect vehicle for difficult trails, yet it is also easy enough to use if you are a complete beginner. If you take frequent overlanding trips and enjoy the challenge of a difficult trail, we would highly recommend this vehicle.

Part of the reason that the Jeep Gladiator is such a good choice is because it gives you a solid vehicle for novices.

When ready it also is very easy to upgrade and has advanced features that you will use as a seasoned overlander.

We recommend going for this type of vehicle if you are going to be working your way up to difficult trails and longer trips. Of course, if you are already an expert you will know this and will probably already have the Jeep Gladiator as your top choice.

Let’s explore the four key points that make the Jeep Gladiator an awesome choice to hit the trails:

Designed For Overlanding:

The Gladiator was designed with overlanding at its heart. This beast is built to withstand the rugged outdoors while providing the comfort of an everyday vehicle.

It will give you solid 4×4 capabilities and this is essential for any trail even if you are a complete beginner. It means that you can rely on the power not just to make trails easier to navigate, it can also help if you get into trouble.

The ground clearance you will get with this vehicle also will make sure that you minimise damage and give you a smooth ride over changing landscapes. Especially if you are on terrain that is frequently changing from rocky to flat. This will make the vehicle easier to drive but it also means that you do not have to worry about damaging the underside of the vehicle.

As well as the 4×4 aspect you will also get something that is durable and reliable. In fact, you will find that the Jeep Gladiator has won several awards over the years. This is a fantastic choice for anyone thinking about delving into overlanding.

Ultimate Power:

The power is one of the best aspects of the Gladiator. If you are a beginner, you will have the power ready for you as you progress to more difficult trails.

If you are an advanced overlander, you will already know how important it is to have the power at your disposal for difficult trails.

Although there are different models of Gladiator available, we do recommend going for the V6 if possible as it will give you the extra power for your overlanding trips.

Storage Made Easy:

Another big reason that the Jeep Gladiator is a great choice is because of the storage options. You will have ample room for your camping gear, food, and all of your other overlanding essentials. Of course, if you need water, you can pair this easily with the Trail’d tank – it is super easy to install and will make sure that you have water when you need it.

Customizability:

As you get more advanced you have a fantastic base with the Jeep Gladiator for upgrades. Whether you are looking to add underbody protection, rooftop tents or any other upgrades it makes it super easy to be able to upgrade your vehicle over time.

For those keen on stepping up their overlanding game, here are some recommended upgrades for the Jeep Gladiator:

Tire Upgrade:

This is probably the easiest modification and one of the most useful especially for beginners. We recommend going for a popular brand such as BF Goodrich as that will give you tires that you can rely on with solid sidewalls and puncture resistance.

Winch Upgrade:

When you are taking on the more difficult trails a winch upgrade is highly recommended. It will give you peace of mind and you will be able to pull yourself out of trouble without having to worry about the winch breaking.

This also works out as an easy upgrade, and we recommend choosing a synthetic rope winch as it will give you a lighter weight and make it easy to use.

Lighting:

If you are thinking of taking on the trails at night, an upgraded lighting system can definitely help with visibility. Some of the upgrades that we recommend are adding spot beams as that will give you better visibility.

The Jeep Gladiator & The Trail’d Tank:

One of the upgrades that we would recommend before anything else is to consider the Trail’d tank. The most important thing that you will need with you on any overlanding trip is water.

The Trail’d tank allows you to get the water that you need and carry in a manner that will not affect the weight or the balance of the vehicle.

With the Gladiator, pairing up with the Trail’d tank is a breeze. This combination caters to all your water requirements on the go. The tank’s installation on the Gladiator is incredibly straightforward and can be mounted in the spare tire location.
